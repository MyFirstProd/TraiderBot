# Environment

Required variables are listed in `.env.example`.

## Security-sensitive

- `SECRET_KEY`
- `ADMIN_API_KEY`
- `BYBIT_API_KEY`
- `BYBIT_API_SECRET`
- `TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN`
- `LITELLM_API_KEY`
- `WHALE_ALERT_API_KEY`
- `TONAPI_KEY`

## Safe defaults

- `PAPER_TRADING=true`
- `TRADING_ENABLED=false`
- `BYBIT_TESTNET=true`
- `DEBUG=false`
